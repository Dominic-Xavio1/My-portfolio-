  • Physics Experiment [Van De Graaf generator]

    A Van de Graaff generator is a device that accumulates electric charge on a hollow metal sphere. It works by using a moving belt to carry charges to the sphere. This creates a high-voltage, low-current electrical source. The generator can be used for various experiments, such as creating sparks or making hair stand on end.
